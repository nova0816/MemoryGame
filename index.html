v1.1.9 (Latest) 2026-06-20
  • Added 11 new educational topics: Colors, Fruits, Shapes, Kitchen, Bathroom (Toilet), Living Room, School, Kindergarten, The Park, The Beach, and Restaurant.
  • Redesigned the Start Screen to support a 12-item category select grid.
  • Added a "🏡 Home" button to allow kids to change topics easily.
  • Created glossy color bubbles and inline SVGs with cute smiley faces for Colors and Shapes categories.
  • Incorporated high-quality vector emojis as card graphics for the other new topics to ensure mobile-compatible, cute stickers.

  • Increased card click zoom scale to 2x (double size) for readability.

  • Added temporary Zoom Pop-out effect: clicking/flipping a card zooms it in by 28% for 1 second.
  • Tightened screen margins and grid gaps on mobile.
v1.1.6 2026-06-20
  • Fixed mobile layout margins where large white borders compressed inner images and words.
  • Reduced card-front padding to 4px and scaled card-word box to 100% of card size.
  • Thinned mobile card borders to 2px.
